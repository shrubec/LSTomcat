About Lottery Simulator

Lottery Simulator is a simple web application designed to demonstrate chances for winning the lottery. It simulates lottery drawing day by day, week by week and comparing randomly generated (drawn) numbers with user selected numbers. Simulator was written by enthusiastic developer who also enjoy playing lottery.

If you are interested in further development of lottery simulator (additional lottery types support, massive simulation support, detailed simulation reports, etc.), feel free to leave a message.

Have fun!
